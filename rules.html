COMMUNITY FIRST

Rules & guidelines.

Keep Supernova SMP welcoming, fair, and fun for everyone. By playing, you agree to follow these rules.

01

Respect everyone

No harassment, hate speech, bullying, threats, or targeted toxicity. Treat every player with basic respect.

02

No cheating or exploits

No hacked clients, X-ray, duping, unfair macros, or abusing bugs. Report exploits instead of using them.

03

No griefing or stealing

Respect claims, builds, farms, and storage. Do not damage or take what belongs to another player.

04

Keep chat appropriate

No spam, excessive caps, advertising, scams, or inappropriate content. Keep public chat friendly.

05

Play fair

PvP, traps, and pranks require consent. Never use deception to cause serious loss or ruin another player’s experience.

06

Build responsibly

Avoid lag machines and destructive builds. Keep names, skins, signs, and creations appropriate for the community.

☀

A few good guidelines

Use common sense, welcome new players, clean up shared areas, and ask staff when you are unsure. Staff may act on behavior that harms the community even when it is not listed word-for-word above.

See a problem? Save evidence and contact the staff team on Discord. Do not start a public argument or retaliate.
